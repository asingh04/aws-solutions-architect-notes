
**st1 / sc 1**

- can not be boot volumes
- size: 125 gb to 16tb
- **st 1** offers:
	- better throughput
	- max throughput: 500 mb/s
	- max IOPS: 500
	- **use case:** big data, data warehousing, logs ingestion
- **sc 1**offers:
	- cold hdd
	- infrequent data access
	- max throughput: 250 mb/s
	- max IOPS: 250
